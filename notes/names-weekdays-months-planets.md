# Weekdays

## Sunday
Sun, Sol or Helios

## Monday
Moon, Luna, or Selene

## Tuesday
Mars or Ares

## Wednesday
Mercury, Mercurius, Hermes
Woden (Odin) - North Germanic / Anglo-Saxons

## Thursday
Jupiter, Jove, or Zeus
Thor / Thunder - Norse god

## Friday
Venus or Aphrodite
Fridge (Frigg's star) - Norse name for venus

## Saturday
Saturn, Saturnus, or Kronos
